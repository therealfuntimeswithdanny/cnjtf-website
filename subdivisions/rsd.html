RSD SUB-UNITS

The list below displays each of our sub-units for RSD

FMU

Field Medical Unit

The FMU is the foundation of RSD. Personnel assigned to this unit respond to routine medical emergencies, administer life-saving aid to civilians and officers, and offer essential medical support during government operations.

Requirements

  • Must be a member of RSD
FPT

Fire Prevention Team

The FPT is tasked with identifying and mitigating fire risks in both operational and civilian zones. FPT personnel work proactively to enforce fire safety protocols, assist in fire suppression efforts, and ensure the safety of structures and personnel in fire-related incidents.

Requirements

  • Must be a member of RSD
ESU

Emergency Support Unit

The ESU is an elite element within RSD. ESU medics are equipped and trained to operate in complex and hazardous environments, including those involving tactical operations and chemical, biological, radiological, and nuclear (CBRN) threats.

Requirements

  • Must be a member of RSD
