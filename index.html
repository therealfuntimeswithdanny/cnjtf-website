About CNJTF

Who we are and what we stand for

The Concord Joint Task Force (CNJTF) is a government-run civil defense organization.
Our goal is to keep the citizens of Concord safe from natural and manmade threats.

Subdivisions

Below is a list of our active subdivisions. The list below displays each one of our available subdivisions

RIU

Rapid Intervention Unit

This division is a specialized field policing unit. Agents in this subdivision are trained to handle high-paced police action such as pursuits, intercepting criminals, and much more.

SUB-UNITS

  • Advanced Patrol Team (APT)
  • Fugitive Prevention and Recovery Service (FPRS)
RSD

Rescue Service Division

This division houses the Fire-rescue and EMS portion of the agency. Agents in this subdivision are trained in fire prevention, special rescue, and medical response instead of law enforcement.

SUB-UNITS

  • Field Medical Unit (FMU)
  • Emergency Service Unit (ESU)
  • Fire Prevention Team (FPT)
SOCOM

Special Operations Command

This division houses the agency's special operations forces. Agents in this subdivision are trained for combat and high-risk operations such as tactical deployments and protection operations.

SUB-UNITS

  • Tactical Response Force (TRF)
  • Advanced Combat Team (ACT)
TAPS

Threat Analysis and Prevention Service

This division is a skilled intelligence service. Agents in this subdivision collect information on pending threats and develop plans to counter them.

SUB-UNITS

  • CLASSIFIED
RRD

Rapid Response Directorate

This division is a special team formed by the Office of the Director. Agents in this subdivision are the best of the best in their field, and report directly to the Agency Director.

SUB-UNITS

  • CLASSIFIED
